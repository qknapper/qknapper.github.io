My Little Pony Bag Tag League 2: Electric Hoove-aloo

The My Little Pony Bag Tag League 2: Electric Hoove-aloo is the second bag tag league of its kind. League members compete in disc golf rounds, swap tags, and climb the rankings to try and reach the top ranked spot! The tournament kicked off on October 30, 2022 and will continue until April 30, 2022. On this page you can view Rankings (scroll) and the Official Rules (scroll). Check back regularly to follow along for the entire competition!
