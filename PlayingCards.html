Playing Cards

Welcome to the showcase of my playing card collection! Sorted by when I officially added them to my personal collection, you'll find images and information - some may even have a video with the overview image. Also, mobile browsers seem to really dislike ahving the background scroll, so sorry about that I guess.

I'm looking to potentially impliment sorting and filtering of what you will soon see on screen. Maybe even a useless API just to see if I can. Tabulated information of this collection is available at qbear.org/PlayingCards/Collection.csv

Currently, some pictures or videos may be missing due to file size - hopefully little to none of these. I am eventually working torwards setting up a personal server for this webisite that won't have the same file size limitations as github. If you want to see something marked "Stored off website" - find me through the info tab and I'll hook you up.
