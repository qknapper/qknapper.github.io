A1 Mario World

A1 Mario World is a Super Mario World romhack that transforms the original SNES classic into a short and refreshing new adventure. While there are plenty of hacks out there that are longer, more polished, harder, and even more enjoyable - A1 Mario World is a unique window into my perspective on the game. Dedicated to the A1 Twins (twitch), this hack is my open letter of appreciation for their infectious excitement that has brough joy to me and many others. A long time ago I mixed and matched some ideas into Super Qbear World (download)/trailer (YouTube). Much more recently, I began refining my skills and putting together new ideas for a future SMW Romhack. A1 Mario World is the result, building on all of my learning experiences and shaping a compelling new platforming challenge.

General Tools

SMW Central: A very large and helpful forum containing many wonderful people. The discussion and code snippets here have been influential in my understanding of adding custom, external content to Super Mario World. Pretty much all of the tools and asm used was sourced from this forum - smwcentral.net

Lunar Magic: The primary Super Mario World level design tool. Lunar Magic is an extremely versatile interface for organizing and adding blocks and sprites in levels and overworlds - smwcentral.net/?p=section&a=details&id=28429

AddMusicK: A tool for inserting custom music (ported as a text file) into Super Mario World - smwcentral.net/?p=section&a=details&id=24994

Asar: A tool for inserting custom asm code into Super Mario World that affects the entire game - smwcentral.net/?p=section&a=details&id=25953

Blockreator: A tool for creating and customizing placeable blocks/objects - smwcentral.net/?p=section&a=details&id=20129

Gopher Popcorn Stew: A tool for inserting custom blocks/objects - smwcentral.net/?p=section&a=details&id=29130

Pixi: A tool for inserting custom sprites - smwcentral.net/?p=section&a=details&id=26026

SPC Play: A music porting tool that emulates the SNES music engine - smwcentral.net/?p=section&a=details&id=28962

Uber ASM: A tool for inserting custom asm code on a level to level basis - smwcentral.net/?p=section&a=details&id=19982
