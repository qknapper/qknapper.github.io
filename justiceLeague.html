Leading the Justice League -

    > Back to Portfolio

"Leading the Justice League" is a brief analysis of leadership qualities intentionally tailored to interpret the unique team dynamics of the superheroes in New 52 incarnation of DC Comic's The Justice League . Specificaly focusing on the character traits of Aquaman, Green Lantern, and Batman, "Leading the Justice League" analyzes both literary and visual elements in order to accurately assess the concept of leadership in this unique comic book media form.

The layout of "Leading the Justice League" was formatted in Adobe InDesign, with images isolated and edited in Photoshop, to intentionally reflect the style of the comic books it discusses while maintaining an intuitive, readable composition.
