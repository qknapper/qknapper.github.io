Score Card Maker

Playing a round of disc golf? Don't have a sheet of paper and think your phone's notes app is lame? This page is a fully flexible score card maker. You can add columns for each player and rows for each hole at will. The score card will also keep track of player's total scores and when you finish the round, you can generate sweet images for you scorecard and round summary (landscape and portrait orientation WOW).

Want to hear some secret features? . . . Come in close . . . If you name the first column of the score card PAR, you can also track player's realtive +/- to that column of the score card. Another secret feature? . . . Oh yeah! See that "Email to Qbear" at the bottom of the score card? You can send in your results to me! That's all the secret features for now, but I think it could be fun to work hard and build in a course map viewer. If you think that would be cool, send me an email!
